# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant] as its code of conduct.

The full text is available at:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported confidentially to the project maintainer at **luiseduardo.boiko@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.
